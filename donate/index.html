Support QAW

Your contributions help realize Queer.Archive.Work’s mission to support alternative artistic practices, experimental publishing, access to creative resources, and community empowerment! Thank you for investing in our vision.

Since our founding in March 2020, QAW has received $2,500 from the sale of Urgency Reader 2, $6,500 in direct donations, and $2,000 in grant funding (City of Providence’s Art, Culture, and Tourism Fund). We were denied funding of $4,000 from the Resist Foundation.

All money received is used for rent ($900/mo), utilities ($50–200/mo), risograph supplies and paper, equipment, residencies, art book fairs, workshops, and compensation for collaborators and artists-in-residence ($500/resident). QAW’s director and its board members do not receive any salary or compensation.
