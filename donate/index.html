Queer.Archive.Work is an exempt organization as described in section 501(c)(3) of the US Internal Revenue Code. All contributions to Queer.Archive.Work are tax-deductible as permitted by law, and are used for general operating costs (rent, electricity), risograph supplies (ink, master rolls, paper, maintenance), and to support programming (Risograph Residencies, Reading Room Workshops, Open House Free Printing). Please contribute any amount—every dollar will help support alternative artistic practices, experimental publishing, access to creative resources, and community empowerment!
