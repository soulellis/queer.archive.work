Providence, RI About



Queer.Archive.Work is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit reading room, publishing studio, and project space serving Providence, RI and beyond.

Who we serve: QAW welcomes all, but priority is given to LGBTQIA+, POC, indigenous, immigrant, differently abled, and other underserved folks. This is a free, open, and accessible community space (please see our code of conduct). Come by to chat about your work, use the risograph printer, make zines, and spend time in the reading room. We’re at 40 Sprague Street, #1G Providence, RI 02907, on the west side of Providence, in a building that used to be a rubber bicycle tire factory. Founder/Director/Attendant: Paul Soulellis (send me a note if you’d like to visit!)
