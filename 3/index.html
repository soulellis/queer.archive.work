QUEER.ARCHIVE.WORK / 3 (2019)



In-progress! This summer, I’m editing and producing QUEER.ARCHIVE.WORK #3, to be launched at Printed Matter’s NY Art Book Fair September 19–22. It’ll be one of several single publications featured as special installations in the dome at MoMA PS1. QUEER.ARCHIVE.WORK #3 contributors: Autumn Knight, Chrysanthemum Tran, Daedalus Li, Kelsey Sucena, Kirslyn Schell-Smith, Lucas LaRochelle, Malcolm Rio, Olive Godlee, Qualeasha Wood, Rin Kim (with Malachi the Moth, Candex Louie, and Bri), Ron Morrison, Sheida Soleimani, Shiv Kotecha, Sophia Le Fraga.
