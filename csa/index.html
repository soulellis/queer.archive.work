Binch/QAW Print CSA*

*Community Supported Art


Updated: August 5, 2023

Inspired by Farm CSAs (community supported agriculture), our Print CSA is a subscription-based share where you will receive a bundle featuring printed goods by our studio community.

The Print CSA subscription is seasonal, where you will receive four shares throughout the year.

Ways to Subscribe:

Full CSA Share

  • $60–$120
  • The print CSA!

On a budget?

Print of the Season

  • $10–$60
  • One special print

A cool institution?

Institutions

  • $150+
  • Be really cool

Poster by Binch Press X QAW members Felipa Estrela and Jules Zuckerberg.

Your subscription supports:

  • Equipment maintenance
  • Purchasing new materials
  • Improving studio accessibility
  • Paying studio staff
  • The continuation of a queer/trans cooperative printshop
