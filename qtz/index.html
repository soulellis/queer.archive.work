Queer & Trans Zinefest 2022


Updated July 29, 2022

Queer/Trans Zinefest (QTZ) is an annual small works festival celebrating the creations of queer, trans, and gender non-conforming artists. This year, we welcome 50 exhibitors from RI and beyond on Sunday, September 4 in Providence, RI at the The Steel Yard 11am–4pm. Come join us! QTZ is totally free and open to the public.
